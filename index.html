Hi! I am working as a postdoctoral fellow in the Department of Mathematics and Statistics, University of North Carolina at Charlotte . My research aims to advance the application of deep learning techniques to solve forward and inverse problems governed by partial differential equations (PDEs). By integrating traditional mathematical methods with modern deep-learning approaches, I aim to improve the efficiency and accuracy of solutions for real-world applications, including image restoration, medical imaging, and related fields.
